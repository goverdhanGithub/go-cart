About Events

IKC is an intercollegiate engineering design competition for undergraduate and graduate engineering students. The object of the competition is to implement good engineering practices, design projects and their related research work. Each participating student will learn, innovate and gain the experience of team work, leadership and the technical skill. The students must function as a team to design, engineer, build, test, promote and compete with a vehicle considering the few sets of rules applied by organizing committee. The organization also aims to provide an opportunity for students to be recognized at a national level for Research and Development careers in automotive engineering . IKC is launched with the approach of introducing International standards of motorsport to young engineering talents who team up to build a machine which not only performs on track but also conquers accuracy in professional design aspects. The difficulty level in static and dynamic events is designed in a manner to challenge the system and the rider of the machine. The event provides platform to engineers to get special mentorship from professionals of Motorsport like Mr.Dhruv Mohite(VW Ameo Cup Champion 2018) & Mr.Mazdayar Vatcha(Multi-time national motorsport champion ) . To maintain the transparency level, Mylaps X2 Transponders will be used to get dynamic test timings on participant’s mobile.
